About Savannah Holdings

Savannah Holdings is a global leader in fuel integrity and marking solutions, dedicated to combating fuel fraud and securing fuel supplies. Through our innovative Fuel Integrity Programmes, we help governments and oil companies protect their revenue and maintain consumer trust. Our services encompass secure chemical markers, extensive field inspections, and a comprehensive tracing system that safeguards fuel quality and ensures compliance with environmental standards.

We are committed to providing a robust and eco-friendly approach to fuel authentication. Our programs detect and deter illegal practices like dilution, smuggling, and unauthorized rebranding, while our advanced analytics and real-time monitoring ensure rapid response to fraud. Savannah Holdings’ solutions benefit governments, oil companies, and consumers by contributing to environmental protection, supporting efficient tax collection, and delivering fuel of the highest quality.

By choosing Savannah Holdings, you partner with a team focused on security, compliance, traceability, and efficiency—setting a new standard for fuel integrity globally.

Our Fuel Solutions

Fuel Marking

Our fuel marking solutions provide a reliable and cost-effective way to ensure fuel integrity and combat fuel adulteration. We work with governments and fuel suppliers to implement robust fuel marking programs that enhance revenue mobilization and conformity.

Fuel Inspection

Our state-of-the-art fuel inspection and monitoring solutions help detect adulteration and ensure compliance with fuel quality standards. We offer advanced technologies and comprehensive services to assist our clients in maintaining fuel integrity and safeguarding their operations.

Fuel Analytics

Our fuel analytics platform provides valuable insights and data-driven intelligence to help our clients optimize their fuel operations, reduce losses, and make informed decisions. We leverage advanced analytics and machine learning to deliver actionable insights and drive sustainable outcomes.

Fuel Traceability

Our comprehensive fuel traceability solutions enable our clients to track the movement of fuel throughout the supply chain, from production to distribution. By providing end-to-end visibility and real-time data, we help our clients enhance supply chain efficiency, minimize losses, and ensure regulatory compliance.
